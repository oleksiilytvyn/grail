All you need for Church.

Grail is a simple, fast, and easy-to-use lyric software. It lets you display songs, bible verses and media, search fast and communicate with other OSC enabled software. Grail is absolutely free software.

photo by Marvin Meyer

Quick search

Search for songs in library or show bible verses from one place.





OSC communication

Cross communicate between application with OSC protocol.





Build-in display

Simple build-in display with cornerpin support and other handy features.
